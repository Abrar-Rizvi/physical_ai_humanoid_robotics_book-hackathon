# Quality Checks

This document outlines the manual and automated quality checks for the book.

## Plagiarism

All content must be original or properly attributed. Before publishing, all chapters should be checked for plagiarism using a commercial or free online tool.

**Procedure:**

1.  Copy the content of a chapter.
2.  Paste it into an online plagiarism checker (e.g., Grammarly, Copyscape, or a free alternative).
3.  Review the results. Any text that is flagged as plagiarized must be rewritten or properly cited.

## Readability

The book aims for a Grade 10-12 readability level.

**Procedure:**

1.  Use an online readability tool (e.g., Hemingway App, Readable.com) to analyze the text.
2.  Review the suggestions to simplify complex sentences and replace jargon where possible.
